Frequently Asked Questions

How do I authenticate a watch?

Every timepiece comes with a certificate of authenticity. Our experts also provide authentication services for pre-owned watches.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn timepieces in original condition with all documentation and packaging.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we partner with premium financing companies to offer flexible payment options for qualified customers.

How long is the warranty?

All new timepieces come with manufacturer warranty, typically 2-5 years depending on the brand. We also offer extended warranty options.
