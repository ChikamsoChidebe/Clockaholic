Our Story

Three decades of horological excellence

The Art of Timekeeping

Founded in 1990, Clockaholic has been at the forefront of luxury timepiece curation for over three decades. What began as a passion project by master horologist Marcus Timewell has evolved into one of the world's most respected watch retailers.

Our Journey

1990

The Beginning

Marcus Timewell opens the first Clockaholic boutique in Geneva, Switzerland.

1995

International Expansion

First international store opens in New York, establishing our global presence.

2000

Digital Revolution

Launch of our online platform, bringing luxury timepieces to customers worldwide.

2010

Exclusive Partnerships

Established exclusive partnerships with premier Swiss manufacturers.

2024

Innovation Continues

Leading the industry with cutting-edge technology and unparalleled service.
